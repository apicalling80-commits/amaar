Our Legacy

THE GENESIS

Where Elegance Meets Extraordinary

Amor Eventz was born from a singular vision: to create a stage where life's most precious celebrations are treated with the reverence of history. What began as a bespoke wedding curation boutique in 2009 has evolved into a sovereign collection of heritage-inspired venues.

Every chandelier, every silk drape, and every carved archway in our halls is a testament to our commitment to timeless luxury. We don't just host events; we preserve legacies through meticulous attention to detail and unmatched hospitality.

"True luxury is found in the meticulous attention to the unseen details."

— FOUNDING PHILOSOPHY
